Cluj-Napoca real estate statistics

This page contains visual representations of Cluj-Napoca's real estate market. The data is fetched from the local major real estate websites.

The available sources (for now) are:

  • piata-az.ro
  • blitz.ro

Price per day

X axis: the date when the information was parsed.

Y axis: the total real estate value for that day (for the parsed estates).

Price per neighborhood

X axis: the neighborhood id. NB: -1 represents the estates that didn't have a neighborhood specified.

Y axis: the mean real estate value for a specific neighborhood.

Price per square meter per neighborhood

X axis: the neighborhood id. NB: -1 represents the estates that didn't have a neighborhood specified.

Y axis: the mean price per square meter per neighborhood.

Price per square meter related to number of rooms.

X axis: total rooms number. NB: 0 represents the estates that didn't have a room number specified.

Y axis: the mean price per square meter related to number of rooms.
